    Q3 Product Strategy

    Scaling our core platform while expanding into enterprise segments

    Strategic Overview

    Our platform has reached a critical inflection point. With 12,000 active teams and accelerating growth in the mid-market segment, we're positioned to capture the enterprise opportunity without sacrificing our core product simplicity.

    This document outlines our three-pronged approach for Q3: deepening platform capabilities, expanding our go-to-market motion, and investing in the infrastructure that will support our next phase of growth.

    Key Insight: Enterprise prospects consistently cite our ease of onboarding as the primary differentiator. Any enterprise features must preserve this advantage.

    Guiding Principles

    • Ship incrementally -- no quarter-long feature bets
    • Measure adoption within 2 weeks of launch
    • Enterprise features must benefit all tiers
    • Maintain sub-200ms p95 response times
